About Kalo Villa

Your home away from home in paradise

Our Story

Welcome to Kalo Villa, your serene sanctuary in the heart of Siargao. Located in the peaceful village of Cabitoonan, just 3.9km from the vibrant General Luna center, we offer an authentic Filipino hospitality experience combined with modern comfort.

Our 5 private beachfront villas were designed to provide the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation. Whether you're here to catch world-class waves, explore pristine islands, or simply unwind by your private pool, Kalo Villa is your gateway to experiencing the magic of Siargao.

What Makes Us Special

Beachfront Paradise

Direct access to pristine beach and your own private pool with stunning ocean views.

Floating Breakfast

Start your day with our signature floating breakfast experience in your private pool.

Wellness Services

In-villa massage treatments and wellness services available on request.

Free Transfers

Complimentary airport shuttle service from Sayak Airport for all guests.

High-Speed WiFi

Stay connected with Starlink high-speed internet throughout your stay.

Family & Pet Friendly

Children of all ages welcome, and your pets stay for free with us.

Meet Joy & The Team

Our dedicated team, led by Joy, is committed to making your Siargao experience unforgettable. With local knowledge and warm Filipino hospitality, we're here 24/7 to help you with anything you need – from arranging island tours to recommending the best local restaurants.

We don't just provide accommodation; we create memories. Our team treats every guest like family, ensuring your stay at Kalo Villa is nothing short of magical.
